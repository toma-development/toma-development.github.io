Our services

01

Property Development

We specialise in transforming undervalued spaces into thriving communities. With a keen eye for design and sustainable practices, we create developments that seamlessly blend into the local landscape while providing modern conveniences. Experience the metamorphosis of a plot into a sought-after property when you choose our expertise.

02

Real Estate

Navigating the property market can be daunting, but with our seasoned real estate team by your side, you're ensured a smooth journey. From sourcing prime properties to ensuring their value appreciation, our expertise is both comprehensive and tailored. Whether you're a first-time buyer or a seasoned investor, we are here to guide, advise, and secure the best opportunities in the market for you.

03

Project Consultation

Whether you're a landowner aiming to maximise your plot's potential or a homeowner envisioning a complete home transformation, our project consultation service is tailored to fit. We offer expert insights, innovative design strategies, and tailored advice to ensure your project's success from conception to completion. Let us partner with you, turning your ideas into a structurally sound and aesthetically pleasing reality.

04

Project Finance

Embarking on a construction or development project can be a significant financial undertaking. Our project financing service provides bespoke solutions to support your vision, ensuring you have the necessary funds at the right stages. With a network of trusted financial partners and a deep understanding of the construction industry's nuances, we offer flexible financing options tailored to your project's scale and requirements. Let us shoulder the financial intricacies so you can focus on bringing your dream project to life.

Who we are

TOMA is a renowned construction and development service committed to reshaping landscapes and lives. Founded on the principles of integrity, creativity, and unwavering dedication, we've been in the business of turning barren lands into thriving communities for over a decade.

We stand out because of our relentless pursuit of excellence, transparency in every step of the process, and a commitment to building sustainable, eco-friendly developments. When you choose TOMA, you choose a partner that goes beyond construction; we create legacies.
